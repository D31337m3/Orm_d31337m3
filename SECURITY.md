## Reporting a Vulnerability

How to report a vulnerability?

 To report a vulnerability, or security concern of any nature, you can send a detailed explaination of the concern by email.
 The address to contact is admin@d31337m3.com, and add "Security Risk" as the subject line to ensure it gets picked up by the proper 
 filters and is forwarded to security admin queue, where it will be reviewed urgently. During review you can expect to have a team
 member reach out to you for questions aboutt the anomoly, or to personally thank you for keeping our platform, and our customers data secure.
(please remember to email "Security Risk" to admin@d31337m3.com, NOT support@d31337m3.com)
